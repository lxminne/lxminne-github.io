Attention Economy

I created this project as a part of a Digital Media Literacy course last semester, with the aim of better understanding some of the underlying systems of the digital media ecosystem. I was also excited to design multimedia elements to showcase my findings-- the goal of the assignment was to create a mock social media campaign, so I created a slide carousel, flowchart, and short video for this project.

Understanding the Attention Economy: a flowchart

When researching the attention economy, I felt that there were many more elements at play than most people realize. To illustrate the web of buying, selling, harvesting and investing in information and attention that makes up the attention economy, I created this flowchart.

Understanding the Attention Economy: a video

I created this video for the project with the goal of summarizing the attention economy, the motivations behind it, and how it affects users in a short, accessible video.
